Phase 1

Discovery & Consultation

Understanding your solar needs. We begin by evaluating your site, energy goals, and eligibility for subsidies like PM-KUSUM Yojana.

  • Site & energy audit
  • Feasibility & ROI analysis
  • Government scheme assistance
  • Customized solar planning
  • Bill, load & wind load analysis
  • PERT chart & project scheduling
Phase 2

Solar Designing & Approvals

Engineering excellence with complete compliance. We handle all technical designs and regulatory liaisoning to ensure a smooth start.

  • EPC system design & layout
  • Load & structural analysis
  • DISCOM registration
  • Net metering & scheme approvals
  • Yield analysis & electrical design
  • Full plant simulation
Phase 3

Procurement & Installation (EPC)

On-time execution with top-tier materials. Our experienced team handles end-to-end procurement and quality-focused installation.

  • Tier-1 modules & inverters (Adani, TATA & more)
  • Civil & electrical work
  • Rooftop, on-ground & hybrid systems
  • A.C./D.C. distribution box setup
  • Complete mechanical installation
  • Cabling, meter & BOS
Phase 4

Installation & Commissioning

Powering your plant with precision. Once installed, we test, synchronise, and energise your plant for optimal performance — evacuating power into your LT panel.

  • Safety & performance testing
  • Grid synchronisation
  • Final documentation & handover
  • PM-KUSUM & MNRE certification
  • Full system testing to design parameters
  • Evacuation into client's LT panel
Phase 5

Operation & Maintenance

Commissioning is only the beginning. Ongoing care keeps your system at maximum capacity for 25+ years — backed by a dedicated O&M team and 12 free engineer visits in year one.

Monthly / Annual Maintenance

Module cleaning, system checkups, wear & tear repairs, inverter servicing and monitoring calibration.

24/7 Monitor & Report

Alert response, monthly production reports, web portal access, email alerts and component monitoring.

Advanced Tools & Repair

De-ionised water cleaning, water reclamation, a dedicated O&M team, and full troubleshoot & repair support.

Specialised Solutions

Project Construction, Engineering & Government Liaisoning
