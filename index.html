Who We Are

Founded on a simple idea: clean power shouldn't be hard to get.

Established in 2020 by Mr. Vivek Jain, Sor Connect began as a 2 MW solar maintenance operation in Rajasthan. Six years on, we manage over 350 MW across 13 states, backed by 130+ in-house engineers, designers and field technicians.

We offer complete, end-to-end services — EPC, Operation & Maintenance, PM-KUSUM Yojana consultation, installation & commissioning, and solar plant design — under a single roof, for residential, commercial, industrial and agricultural clients alike.

Our Process

From concept to commissioning, in five disciplined phases.

Every Sor Connect project moves through the same proven sequence — so quality stays consistent whether the plant is 50 kW on a rooftop or 2 MW on open land.

01

Discovery & Feasibility

Site inspection, bill & load analysis, wind load study, ROI and subsidy eligibility.

02

Design & Approvals

EPC layout, structural design, DISCOM registration, net metering & scheme approvals.

03

Procurement & Installation

Tier-1 modules and inverters, civil & electrical work, rooftop, on-ground or hybrid builds.

04

Commissioning

Full system testing, grid sync, final documentation, PM-KUSUM & MNRE certification.

05

Asset Management

Remote monitoring, preventive & corrective maintenance, power audits, long-term AMC.
